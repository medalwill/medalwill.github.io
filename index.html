About Me

I'm currently a Vision Algorithm Researcher in AIPD, TEG, Tencent.

I got my Master's degree and Bachelor's degree in the School of Informatics at Xiamen University, supervised by Xuan Cheng (opens new window).

In 2023.06 -2023.12, I served as a research intern at Virtual Human Group (opens new window) of NetEase Fuxi AI Lab (opens new window), working with Yu Ding (opens new window).

My research interests lie in Computer Vision and Computer Graphics. I'm particularly interested in virtual human and AIGC.
